While working with Atech, I helped to update the website, both in design and tech. I was the lead designer for a partial-site redesign. My role was to create a landing page that enticed potential customers and to simplify the application process.

To accomplish this task, I used Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for prototyping and mockups. I used HTML, CSS (Sass) and JavaScript, to build these pages.
